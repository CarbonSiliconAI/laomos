# Analysis Criteria for Mia Polymarket Trader

## Performance Metrics to Track

* **Trading P&L**: Calculate the total profit and loss of the agent's trades over a specified time period.
* **Win/Loss Ratio**: Track the ratio of successful to unsuccessful trades.
* **Average Trade Profit**: Calculate the average profit per trade.
* **Maximum Drawdown**: Monitor the maximum drawdown (peak-to-trough decline) of the agent's portfolio.
* **Sharpe Ratio**: Calculate the Sharpe ratio, which measures risk-adjusted return.

## Quality Criteria for Outputs

* **Trade Execution**: Ensure that trades are executed accurately and in a timely manner.
* **Risk Management**: Verify that the agent's risk management strategies are effective in minimizing losses.
* **Market Analysis**: Evaluate the accuracy and relevance of the agent's market analysis and predictions.
* **Automated Trading**: Confirm that the agent's automated trading system is functioning correctly and without errors.

## Success/Failure Indicators

* **Positive P&L**: The agent's trading P&L is positive over a specified time period.
* **Win Rate**: The agent's win rate is above a specified threshold (e.g., 60%).
* **Maximum Drawdown**: The agent's maximum drawdown is below a specified threshold (e.g., 10%).

## Optimization Targets

* **Maximize Profit**: Optimize the agent's trading strategy to maximize profit while minimizing risk.
* **Minimize Losses**: Optimize the agent's trading strategy to minimize losses while maintaining a positive P&L.
* **Improve Win Rate**: Optimize the agent's trading strategy to improve the win rate while reducing the maximum drawdown.
